A 3D scene built through OpenGL

I got my inspiration from my trip to Chester. I was attracted by the local cathedral. During the visit, I was inspired to create a scene in the style of a cathedral garden.

Executable available at: Github

My scene consists of walls, floors, arches, a fountain, and a statue. Among them, the arches are procedurally generated. I created a basic arch shape and a cylinder. Then combined them to obtain different architectural structures. The base of the fountain is my hand craft model. Statue(horse) is the imported from obj file. Also, I created a texture for the smaller floor.

Procedurally generated object

This object is composed of two parts: columns (cylinders) and arches. Each column is composed of four cylinders of different sizes. The arch used a curve to build one of the sides, and then calculated another straight side through a function. After creating two edges, two faces (front and back) can be generated. For side faces, I need to arrange the vertices on the curved and straight edges in order, and then use a function to generate side faces.
